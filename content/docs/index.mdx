Welcome to the official UnifiedHytale documentation! This guide will help you get started whether you're a player looking for content or a creator ready to publish.

## What is UnifiedHytale?

UnifiedHytale is a curated marketplace where creators can share and sell their Hytale content. We provide:

- **Secure Downloads** - All files are virus-scanned before approval
- **Creator Tools** - Easy upload, version management, and analytics
- **Community Features** - Reviews, ratings, and discussions
- **Server Listings** - Find and promote Hytale servers
- **API Access** - Build integrations with our platform
