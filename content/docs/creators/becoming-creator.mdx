Ready to share your Hytale creations with the world? Here's how to become a creator on UnifiedHytale.

## Requirements

To become a creator, you need:

- A verified UnifiedHytale account
- Agreement to our [Creator Terms](https://unifiedhytale.com/creator-terms)
- Original content ready to upload

## Application Process

1. **Create an Account** - [Sign up](https://unifiedhytale.com/signup) if you haven't already
2. **Verify Email** - Confirm your email address
3. **Go to Dashboard** - Visit your [Dashboard](https://unifiedhytale.com/dashboard)
4. **Apply** - Click "Become a Creator"
5. **Fill Form** - Tell us about your plans
6. **Wait for Review** - Usually 24-48 hours

## Application Tips

Your application should include:

- What type of content you plan to create
- Your experience with Hytale modding
- Links to previous work (if any)
- Why you want to join UnifiedHytale

<Callout type="info">
Quality over quantity! We're looking for creators who will contribute valuable content to the community.
</Callout>

## After Approval

Once approved, you can:

- Upload unlimited projects
- Access creator analytics
- Receive community support
- Apply for verified status

## Verified Creator Status

Verified creators receive:

- Verified badge on profile
- Priority moderation queue
- Featured placement opportunities
- Direct support channel

To qualify for verification:

- Active for 3+ months
- 5+ approved projects
- Positive community standing
- Consistent quality content

## Creator Guidelines

All creators must follow our [Community Guidelines](https://unifiedhytale.com/guidelines):

- Original content only
- Accurate descriptions
- Proper categorization
- Responsive to user feedback
- No malicious code
