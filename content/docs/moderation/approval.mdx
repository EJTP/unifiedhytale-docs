Understanding the approval process helps you get your content published faster.

## Approval Steps

### Step 1: Submission

When you click "Submit for Review":
- Project enters moderation queue
- You receive a confirmation email
- Status changes to "Pending Review"

### Step 2: Automated Checks

Our system automatically checks:

- **Virus Scan** - Files scanned for malware
- **File Validation** - Correct format and size
- **Required Fields** - All necessary info provided
- **Duplicate Check** - Not a re-upload of existing content

### Step 3: Manual Review

A moderator reviews:

- Description accuracy and quality
- Proper categorization
- Screenshots/media appropriateness
- Compliance with guidelines
- Copyright and licensing

### Step 4: Decision

**Approved:**
- Project goes live immediately
- Email notification sent
- Appears in search results

**Needs Changes:**
- Specific feedback provided
- Make requested changes
- Resubmit for review

**Rejected:**
- Reason explained in email
- Serious violations may affect account standing

## Tips for Fast Approval

1. **Complete descriptions** - Include all relevant info
2. **Proper categorization** - Choose accurate types/categories
3. **Quality media** - Clear screenshots and icons
4. **Original content** - No stolen or unauthorized work
5. **Follow guidelines** - Read our [Community Guidelines](https://unifiedhytale.com/guidelines)

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was incorrect:

1. Reply to the rejection email
2. Explain why you disagree
3. Provide additional context
4. Wait for review (48-72 hours)

<Callout type="info">
Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators who weren't involved in the original decision.
</Callout>
