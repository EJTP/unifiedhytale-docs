---

Getting started with UnifiedHytale is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account.

## Sign Up

1. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner of the website
2. Enter your email address, username, and password
3. Check your email for a verification link (check spam folder if needed)
4. Click the verification link to activate your account

<Callout type="info">
Choose your username carefully - you cannot change it later!
</Callout>

## Profile Setup

After creating your account, customize your profile in [Settings](https://unifiedhytale.com/settings):

### Avatar
Upload a profile picture (max 2MB, PNG/JPG/WebP format). Square images work best.

### Bio
Tell others about yourself. Your bio supports markdown formatting:
- **Bold text** with `**text**`
- *Italic text* with `*text*`
- [Links](https://example.com) with `[text](url)`

### Social Links
Connect your other profiles:
- Discord username
- Twitter/X handle
- GitHub profile
- Personal website

### Email Notifications
Configure what updates you want to receive:
- New followers
- Comments on your projects
- Download milestones
- Newsletter and announcements

## Account Security

We recommend enabling these security features:

- **Strong Password** - Use a unique password with at least 12 characters
- **Email Verification** - Keep your email verified for account recovery
- **Two-Factor Authentication** - Coming soon!
